Who We Are

Incorporated in November 2008, First Investment Development Company (FIDC) is a real estate development and investment company with a focus on the residential and commercial sector of the Nigerian market.

We are made up of a team of dedicated and experienced professionals with proven track record in real estate development, investment management and finance. With expertise cutting across all phases of the development cycle, our multi-disciplinary professionals are able to conceptualise, design, construct and deliver real estate schemes to the required taste and specifications of our clients.

Our approach to problem-solving is innovative, creative, imaginative and entrepreneurial, and we always seek out investment or development opportunities where value can be created.
